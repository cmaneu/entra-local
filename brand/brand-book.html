Identity

Fluent-mimic. Entra Local deliberately resembles the real Entra/Azure experience — white surfaces, restrained depth, ~4px corners, a Segoe type voice, Azure communication-blue actions — while the persistent amber LOCAL EMULATOR badge makes it unmistakable that this is a local sandbox, never production and never Microsoft.
