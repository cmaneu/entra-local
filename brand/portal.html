Accessibility / aria-live note for Baracus: mount one polite role="status" region and one assertive role="alert" region at the app shell root (visually hidden, .sr-only). Route copy/save/seed/reset confirmations and toast text through the polite region; route load/mutation failures through the assertive region. Dialogs and drawers are role="dialog" aria-modal="true", trap focus (first focusable / a sensible default gets autofocus), return focus to the invoking control on close, and dismiss on Esc + scrim click. Every control has a visible 2px #0078D4 focus ring (never removed). Status is always color + icon + text, never color alone.
